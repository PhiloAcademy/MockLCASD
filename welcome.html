Welcome

At St. David’s, we believe God has a royal calling for every child: to grow in wisdom, stature, and the grace of God and man (Luke 2:52).

Our Mission

Provide an education rooted in Holy Scripture, the Western heritage, and the pursuit of excellence.

Our Vision

Raise learned disciples of Jesus who confess the faith with joy and serve with competence, humility, and virtue.
